Pakistan's Game Developer's Conference

Pakistan's Game Developer's Conference The Pakistan Game Developers Conference, organized by the Department of Computer Game Development at Air University, is the premier event that brings together the vibrant community of game developers, enthusiasts, and industry professionals in Pakistan. PGDC serves as the largest platform for game developers to showcase their creativity, ideas, projects, and final year creations.

Project Exhibition

Showcase your game development projects and prototypes to a diverse audience of industry experts, fellow developers, and gaming enthusiasts.

Game Jam

Dive into the exciting world of game development in a high-energy game jam, where participants work in teams to create unique and innovative games within a limited timeframe

FYP (Final Year Project) Exhibition

Final year students can display their innovative and groundbreaking game development projects, allowing them to gain valuable feedback and recognition.

Speakers

Gain insights from renowned industry professionals, game designers, and experts through inspiring talks and panel discussions.
